Greek Soul, Hove Heart

We don't write stories—We bake them.

Born from a village kitchen in Athens and perfected on the South Coast, our mission is simple: Authentic Greek flavors, handmade every morning.

  • 24-Hour Sourdough: Slow-fermented for that perfect tang.
  • Hand-Folded Phyllo: Crispy, golden, and buttered by hand.
  • Wild Greek Honey: Imported directly from Aegean cooperatives.
